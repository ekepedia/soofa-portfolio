How to Sign Up

Congratulations on being selected as a top 20 realtor. Here are some simple instructions to get you started with your profile:



First, navigate to the top of the screen and click on "Login"



Then click "Sign Up"



Make sure to fill out all of the fields correctly and to read the Standalone License Agreement



You're done. At this page you can upload a custom photo (make sure it is a square image) and edit your about description. Make sure to check your portal periodically to catch new features we'll be adding over time!
